About the Wiki Archive

Witchery Wiki Preservation Project

This is a preserved static archive of the original Witchery Mod Wiki. It was originally hosted on Google Sites but was deleted after Google updated the platform. This archive was made from a backup I took in 2021 before the site was shut down and has all 400+ pages of documentation still intact.

What is Witchery?

Witchery is an amazing Minecraft mod made by Emoniph in 2014. Unfortunately, Emoniph disappeared from the internet many years ago and nobody has been able to contact him since. I do not own any rights to Witchery, and this website is merely a tribute to the mod after 10 years of magic.
